Equipment Use & Return Agreement

Equipment Use and Return Agreement

By registering here, I, the undersigned parent(s) or legal guardian(s), hereby guarantee the safekeeping and timely return or reimbursement of the replacement value of any and all equipment issued to my child by E.C. Hurricanes Youth Football & Cheer. I will not modify or mark the equipment in any manner to make it unusable or undesirable for future participants.

Code of Conduct

Code of Conduct — Fans, Parents & Players

The Ideology of Youth Sports Including This Program Is To Promote Good Understanding And Fundamental Knowledge Of The Sport. It Is Critical That Good Sportsmanship Is Maintained Both On And Off The Field.

Fans shall: Not criticize players/coaches in front of spectators; Accept officials' decisions as fair; Not criticize opposing teams; Refrain from using abusive or profane language at any time; Not be under the influence of alcohol or illegal substances; Not interfere with coaching staff before, during, or after games.

Athletes Code: I will emphasize sportsmanship, ethical conduct and fair play. I will not use profanity or "talk trash" before, during or after any game. I will not use drugs, alcohol, or tobacco.

Parent's Code: I will support my child's team and teach the value of commitment. I will not criticize officials or direct abuse toward them. I will not intrude onto the field or yell from the bleachers at coaches, referees or administration.

ANY VIOLATION of the Code of Conduct risks the future participation of the child in the program for up to three years.

Concussion / MTBI Acknowledgement

Mild Traumatic Brain Injury (MTBI) / Concussion Annual Statement

I acknowledge that:

  • My organization has provided educational materials including the CDC Concussion fact sheet on what a concussion is.
  • I have fully disclosed to the staff any prior medical conditions and will also disclose any future conditions.
  • A concussion is a brain injury, which I am responsible for reporting to the team physician, athletic trainer, coach, parent volunteer, or official.
  • I will not return to play in a game or practice if I have received a blow to the head or body that results in concussion related symptoms.
  • I will not return to play until my symptoms have resolved AND I have written clearance from a qualified health care professional.
  • Following concussion, the brain needs time to heal and I am much more likely to have a repeat concussion if I return to play before my symptoms resolve.
